Fried Rice Recipe

Fried rice is a popular dish that originated in China and has become a staple in many Asian cuisines. The dish is made by stir-frying cooked rice with various ingredients such as vegetables, meat, seafood, eggs, and sauces. The ingredients are typically cooked separately and then combined with the rice in a wok or a large frying pan, where they are stir-fried over high heat until the rice is crispy and infused with the flavors of the other ingredients. Fried rice is a versatile dish that can be customized to suit individual tastes and preferences. It can be made with a variety of ingredients, and the level of spiciness can be adjusted to taste.

Fried rice is a simple and delicious way to use up leftover rice and is a great option for a quick and easy meal. It can be made with almost any protein and vegetable, making it a great way to incorporate healthy ingredients into your diet. The dish is also very budget-friendly and can be made with pantry staples that you likely already have on hand. Whether you are looking for a quick lunch, a weeknight dinner, or a party dish, fried rice is a tasty and satisfying option that is sure to please everyone at the table.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked white rice, preferably day-old
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: diced chicken, shrimp, or tofu for added protein

Steps to cook!

  • Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and onion and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  • Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until they are heated through and slightly softened.
  • Push the vegetables to the side of the pan and add the beaten eggs to the other side. Scramble the eggs until fully cooked, then mix them together with the vegetables.
  • Add the cooked rice to the skillet and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, breaking up any clumps with a spatula.
  • Add the soy sauce and sesame oil to the skillet and stir to evenly coat the rice and vegetables. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  • If desired, add diced chicken, shrimp, or tofu to the skillet and stir-fry until cooked through.
  • Serve hot and enjoy!
